The history of tea leaf in the world

Tea has a rich history that spans over 5,000 years. Legend says it was discovered in China by Emperor Shen Nong in 2737 BCE when a tea leaf blew into a pot of boiling water. From there, tea spread across Asia, gaining popularity in Japan, India, and beyond.

By the 17th century, tea reached Europe, quickly becoming a beloved drink worldwide. Today, tea is enjoyed by millions globally, continuing its legacy as one of the most cherished beverages in history.
